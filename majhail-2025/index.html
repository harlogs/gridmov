Majhail - 2025

Added on: May 1, 2025

Language: Hindi

Year: 2025

Categories: Thriller

Majhail is a Punjabi-language action film directed by Dheeraj Kedarnath Rattan.[1][2][3] The film is written by Dheeraj Kedarnath Rattan and Manila Rattan.[4] It stars Dev Kharoud and Roopi Gill in lead roles and Guggu Gill, Dheeraj Kumar, Vadda Grewal, and Hobby Dhaliwal in supporting roles.[5][6] The film released in theatres on 31 January 2025.[7]

The film was filmed at multiple locations across Punjab, including Nabha, Mohali, Kurali, Morinda, and Nandpur Kalaur.[1] Principal photography for the film commenced on 14 May 2024 and concluded in September 2024.[8][9][10]
